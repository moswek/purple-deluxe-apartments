# **App Name**: Purple Majesty Suites

## Core Features:

- Suite Immersion: Interactive suite showcase with high-resolution images and 360° virtual tours to allow visitors to explore each suite.
- Effortless Booking: A streamlined booking system that guides guests through available dates, suite options, and instant confirmation.
- Explore Kampala: Integrated map functionality showcasing nearby attractions and exclusive Kampala experiences.
- Contact Concierge: Contact portal for premium support.
- AI Concierge: AI-driven chatbot tool providing personalized recommendations for local experiences.
- Our Legacy: Rich, dynamic About page highlighting the exclusivity and history of Purple Deluxe Apartments.
- Amenities Showcase: Dedicated pages that showcase all the amenities offered, with icons representing them.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#4A148C) to evoke royalty and luxury.
- Background color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to provide a muted, elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) to add a touch of opulence and highlight important elements.
- Body text: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel; suitable for body text
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif with personality, and a style aligned to fashion, art, and design; suitable for headlines
- Custom-designed, minimalist icons with a gold or white outline against purple backgrounds.
- Full-screen parallax scrolling effects with strategic use of white space to create a sense of depth and luxury.
- Smooth, subtle animations on button hover and page transitions for an interactive experience.
